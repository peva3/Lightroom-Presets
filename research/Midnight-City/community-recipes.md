# Midnight City — Community Recipes

## Source

## Published Slider Values
Exposure: -0.10 | Contrast: +28 | Highlights: -30 | Shadows: +22 | Whites: +10 | Blacks: -15 | Clarity: +20 | Dehaze: +12 | Saturation: +8

## Description
Midnight City preset for urban night photography. Combines high contrast with clarity and dehaze for the sharp, punchy night city look. Designed across Tokyo, London, and New York streets to enhance neon purples, glowing cyans, and warm amber streetlights while keeping asphalt and buildings dark, gritty, and textured.

Drop Highlights to recover blown neon signs. Push Clarity + Dehaze for sharp wet street reflections. Cool Temp slightly to turn yellow streetlights into warm gold against deep blue shadows.
