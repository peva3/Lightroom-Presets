# Midnight City — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Dark, moody urban nights
- Aggressive clarity + dehaze for crisp neon definition
- Slightly underexposed for nighttime atmosphere
- Warm highlight bias against cool blue shadows
- Classic city-at-night color contrast

## Usage
Best for: Low-light city streets, neon signs, rain reflections, subway stations, moody urban portraits. Works best in environments with defined light sources.
