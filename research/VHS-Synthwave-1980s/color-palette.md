## Color Theory: The Synthwave Look

### The Split-Complementary Foundation
The synthwave palette is fundamentally built on a **magenta-cyan split-complementary** relationship:
- **Dominant:** Magenta/Pink (`#FF2975`, `#F222FF`)
- **Complement:** Cyan/Teal (`#04C4CA`, `#00FFFF`)
- **Secondary Warmth:** Orange/Gold (`#FF901F`, `#FFD319`)

### Why This Works
- Magenta and cyan are ~180° apart on the color wheel (direct complements)
- Adding orange creates a triadic-like harmony
- The deep purple/blues serve as a neutral dark base
- High saturation neon against deep blacks creates the "glow" effect

### Chromatic Contrast
The aesthetic relies on **simultaneous contrast**:
- Cyan elements appear brighter against dark purple backgrounds
- Magenta "bleeds" optically when placed next to cyan
- Warm orange creates depth against cool blue shadows

## Common Synthwave Color Schemes

### Scheme A: "Miami Nights" (Outrun Classic)
```
Background:    #0D0221 (deep purple-black)
Primary:       #FF2975 (hot pink)
Secondary:     #04C4CA (cyan)
Accent:        #FFD319 (yellow)
```

### Scheme B: "Stranger Things" (Horror Synth)
```
Background:    #0A0A0A (near black)
Primary:       #DE004E (deep red)
Secondary:     #1B2853 (navy blue)
Accent:        #F3CE75 (pale gold warmth)
```

### Scheme C: "Drive" (Neo-Noir Synth)
```
Background:    #1A0533 (dark purple)
Primary:       #FF901F (orange neon)
Secondary:     #F222FF (magenta)
Accent:        #8C1EFF (blue-violet)
```

### Scheme D: "VHS Degraded"
```
Background:    #2D1B69 (faded purple)
Primary:       #C78BBA (washed magenta)
Secondary:     #8BA4C7 (washed cyan-blue)
Accent:        #D4C5A9 (washed beige/cream)
```

## Applying to Lightroom

### HSL Slider Targets for Synthwave

| Color | Hue Shift | Saturation | Luminance |
|-------|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Red | +15 toward magenta | +20 | -10 |
| Orange | -10 toward red-orange | +25 | -5 |
| Yellow | -5 toward orange | +15 | +10 |
| Green | -40 toward yellow (minimize) | -60 | -20 |
| Aqua | +10 toward cyan | +30 | +15 |
| Blue | -15 toward cyan | -10 | -20 |
| Purple | +20 toward magenta | +25 | -10 |
| Magenta | +10 toward pink | +35 | +5 |

### Split Toning
- **Highlights:** Magenta/Pink (Hue 290-320, Saturation 20-40)
- **Shadows:** Cyan/Teal (Hue 180-200, Saturation 15-30)
- **Balance:** Shifted toward highlights (+30 to +60)

### Calibration Panel
- **Red Primary:** Hue +30, Saturation +15
- **Green Primary:** Hue -40, Saturation -30
- **Blue Primary:** Hue -20, Saturation +10
