## Market Observations

1. **Cinestill 800T is the single most monetized film emulation** — nearly every preset shop has a Cinestill pack
2. **Halation is the differentiator** — packs that include halation brushes/actions charge premium prices
3. **The market is saturated** — Etsy alone shows dozens of Cinestill presets from individual creators
4. **Desktop vs Mobile split** — Most serious packs target Lightroom Classic/CC; mobile DNG presets are common for casual users
5. **Price range**: $3 (Etsy/Gumroad individuals) to $199+ (Dehancer professional plugin)
6. **Photoshop dependency**: Most packs that claim accurate halation require Photoshop for the halation action; only Dehancer and some Lightroom brush packs can do it within a single app
