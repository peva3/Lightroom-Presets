# Kodak Ektachrome 200 — Aesthetic Characteristics for Lightroom Presets

## The Film

Kodak Ektachrome 200 (E-6, daylight balanced at 5500K) was Kodak's mid-speed professional slide film. Positioned between Ektachrome 100 and the high-speed Ektachrome 400X, it offered a useful 1-stop advantage over ISO 100 films while still maintaining professional-grade image quality. It was the "general purpose" slide film that balanced speed, grain, and color accuracy. This document serves as a reference for creating a Lightroom preset that emulates Ektachrome 200.

## Core Aesthetic

### 1. Balanced Professional Slide
- **Moderate-to-high contrast** — more contrast than negative films, slightly less than E100
- **Neutral color balance** with a slight warmth compared to the cool-leaning E100
- **Balanced saturation** — professional but not vivid (unlike E100VS)
- Designed for consistent results across varied lighting conditions

### 2. Color Palette by Hue

| Hue | Ektachrome 200 Rendering |
|-----|--------------------------|
| **Blue (sky)** | Clean, slightly warm-tinted blue — less cyan than early Ektachromes |
| **Blue (shadow)** | Neutral-cool, not aggressively blue |
| **Red** | True saturated red, slightly warm |
| **Green (foliage)** | Natural green with slight yellow bias |
| **Skin tones** | Warmer and more forgiving than E100 — better for portraits |
| **Yellow/Orange** | Warm, golden — good for sunset/golden hour |
| **Whites** | Clean neutral white |

### 3. Contrast & Dynamic Range
- **Moderate-high contrast** — professional slide film character
- **RMS ~11-12** — slightly more grain than E100, typical for ISO 200 slide
- **Dynamic range ~7 stops** — standard for E-6 slide films
- Good highlight separation but shadows go to black quickly

### 4. Key Differentiator: The Warm-Neutral Balance
Unlike the aggressively cool E100 (2018), Ektachrome 200 has a more balanced, slightly warmer rendering:
- Less cyan in the blue channel
- Warmer midtones (more golden than cold)
- Better skin tone rendering
- More "natural" looking for general photography

## Comparison: Ektachrome 200 vs E100

| Attribute | Ektachrome 200 | E100 (2018) |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Contrast | Moderate-high | High |
| Shadow bias | Neutral-cool | Strong cool-blue |
| Skin tones | Warm, forgiving | Cool, pale |
| Blue rendering | Clean warm-blue | Cyan-leaning |
| Saturation | Moderate | Moderate-high |
| Grain | RMS ~11-12 | RMS 8 |
| Best for | General photography, portraits | Architecture, editorial |
