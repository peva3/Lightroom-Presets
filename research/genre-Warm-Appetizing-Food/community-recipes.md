# Warm Appetizing Food Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Warm Appetizing Food Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/FoodPhotography: warmth is the universal preference — food shot under cool light looks unappetizing. cinemorapresets.com: Food Warm formula uses warm split toning, selective saturation on warm colors. Shotkit food editing guide: boost reds/oranges, desaturate greens, warm shadows.
