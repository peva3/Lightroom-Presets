## Description
Stage Lights preset for concert and stage photography. The highest contrast and clarity in the collection, designed to handle the extreme dynamic range of stage lighting. Maximum clarity + dehaze combination for sharp stage shots despite atmospheric haze. Aggressive highlight recovery for spotlights prevents blown-out areas on performers. Slight warmth addition counterbalances typically cool stage lighting. Deep blacks for dramatic stage atmosphere.
