# Golden Grid Architecture — Lightroom Preset Settings & Recipes

## Source

This preset was provided as an exact XMP file by the repo maintainer. Its origin is architectural photography editing — a warm golden-hour look designed for building and cityscape photography.

## Design Notes

- The |Vibrance(20)−Saturation(5)| = 15 gap is intentional — midtone pop for architectural textures on a controlled global saturation base
- Yellow Hue -10 shifts warm tones away from green toward orange/gold
- Green Sat -20 suppresses foliage so warm building materials dominate
- The vignette is stronger than typical landscape presets (-35) — architectural compositions benefit from corner falloff
- No grain — crisp lines are the priority for architecture
