# Vibrant Color Pop — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Maximum color impact
- High saturation + vibrance for vivid colors
- Full-spectrum HSL enhancement (red, orange, yellow, blue)
- Moderate clarity for color definition
- Gentle contrast preserves tonal detail in saturated areas

## Usage
Best for: Travel photography, festivals, fashion, food photography, any scene where bold, punchy colors are the focus.
