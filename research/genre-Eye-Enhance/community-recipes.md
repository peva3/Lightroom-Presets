# Eye Enhance Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Eye Enhance Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. Peter McKinnon eye pop technique: Radial Gradient on each eye with Clarity +25, Dehaze +10, Exposure +0.3. r/WildlifePhotography: catchlights make or break wildlife portraits. cinemorapresets.com: Wildlife Portrait formula uses moderate clarity, catchlight enhancement.
