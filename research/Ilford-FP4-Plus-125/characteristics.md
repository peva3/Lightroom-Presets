# Ilford FP4 Plus 125 — Aesthetic Characteristics

## Overview

Ilford FP4 Plus 125 is the classic medium-speed traditional-grain B&W film. It combines fine grain with a beautiful tonal scale and moderate contrast — a versatile workhorse beloved by landscape, portrait, and fine-art photographers since its introduction. FP4+ represents the "golden mean" between the ultra-fine Pan F and the faster HP5+.

## Grain

- **RMS Granularity ~10** — fine, visible only at high magnification
- Traditional cubic-grain structure with smooth, even distribution
- Noticeably finer than HP5+ but grainier than Pan F
- At 35mm: visible texture at 11×14", smooth at 8×10"
- In 120: essentially grainless at normal print sizes

## Contrast & Tonal Characteristics

- **Moderate native contrast** (gamma ~0.60-0.65)
- Long, smooth tonal scale
- Excellent highlight separation — holds detail well into Zone VIII-IX
- Good shadow detail — Zone II-III retains visible texture
- More forgiving exposure latitude than Pan F (~±2 stops)

## Spectral Sensitivity

- Full panchromatic with balanced red/green/blue response
- Natural skin tone rendering — neither brightened nor darkened
- Blue skies: moderately dark, good cloud separation
- Green foliage: rich midtone rendering
- Overall: the most "neutral" Ilford B&W spectral response

## Format Behavior

| Format | Character |
|--------|-----------|
| 35mm | Fine grain, excellent sharpness |
| 120 | Near-grainless, gallery-quality tonality |
| 4×5 / 8×10 | Ultimate resolution, perfect tonal scale |

## Developer Effects

| Developer | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| ID-11/D-76 1:1 | Reference standard — balanced, fine grain |
| Perceptol | Ultra-fine grain, fuller shadow detail |
| Rodinal 1:50 | Higher acutance, crisp grain |
| HC-110 Dil B | Snappy contrast, slight grain enhancement |

## Best Uses

- General-purpose outdoor photography
- Landscape with smooth skies
- Portraiture (especially 120 format)
- Architecture and fine detail
- Any situation wanting the "classic film" look without dominant grain
