# Soft Dreamy Pastel — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Dreamy, ethereal, pastel rendering
- Strong negative clarity for soft romantic bloom
- Low contrast maintains gentle tonal transitions
- Slightly elevated exposure for airy feel
- Warm tint for golden-hour softness
- Lifted, matte-like quality

## Usage
Best for: Wedding photography, fashion editorials, dreamy portraits, romantic scenes, any image seeking an ethereal, pastel quality.
