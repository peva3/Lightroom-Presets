## Aesthetic Profile

### The "Dream Look"
This preset creates a soft, glowing, vintage cinematic dream aesthetic. The image feels suspended in a warm golden-amber glow with an ethereal airiness. It's a high-key variant of the cinematic look — bright, lifted, and luminous rather than dark and moody.

### Core Visual Identity
- **Ethereal airiness:** The entire tonal range is compressed toward the bright end. Blacks are massively lifted (+40), creating a fog-like wrap. Whites are pulled down (-43) so nothing clips harshly. The result is a soft, floating aesthetic with no harsh shadows or blown highlights.
- **Golden-amber glow:** The midtone and highlight color grade (H74°/H65°) bathes the image in a warm golden light. This isn't a subtle warmth — the midtone saturation at 10% and highlight at 22% are assertive, creating an unmistakable "golden hour" tonality that persists even on images shot in flat light.
- **Extreme Vibrance pop:** The defining slider of this preset is Vibrance +64 against Saturation -9. This creates maximum midtone colorfulness on a globally desaturated base — skin tones glow warmly while shadows remain muted. Colors appear selectively pumped rather than globally boosted.
- **Cinematic lifted curve:** The tone curve lifts the black point to 15 (significant) and compresses highlights to 235. The master curve is: 0,15 / 45,45 / 128,128 / 192,196 / 255,235. This is the classic "matte film" curve architecture.
- **Blue channel shadow lift:** The per-channel blue curve lifts blue in shadows (0,10 / 64,60) while leaving red/green neutral. This introduces a subtle blue-cyan coolness into the lifted shadows, creating natural color contrast against the warm golden highlights — the classic "warm/cool split" done at the curve level rather than split toning.

### Color Palette

**Warm tones (red, orange, yellow):**
- Orange hue -46 (aggressive shift toward yellow) — the biggest single hue move. This transforms warm skin tones from peachy-orange into a golden-yellow warmth. Combined with -9 orange saturation, skin is desaturated but golden.
- Yellow sat +15 (boost yellows) + lum +39 (massively brightened) — creates the "airy golden glow" aesthetic. Yellow tones (sunlight, highlights, golden hour) become luminous and expansive.
- Red lum +17 brightens red tones for a softer, less aggressive warmth.

**Cool tones (green, aqua, blue):**
- Green hue +17 toward cyan-olive, sat -15, lum +15 — greens become muted, olive-toned, and slightly luminous. Foliage reads as dreamy soft green rather than aggressive neon.
- Aqua hue +15 toward blue, sat -26, lum +16 — desaturated teal that brightens toward blue
- Blue hue -20 (toward cyan), sat -18, lum -30 — deep rich cinematic blues with darkened luminance for sky/water drama. This is the "blockbuster blue" move.

**Color Grade (3-way wheels):**
- Midtone: H74° (golden yellow-green), Sat 10% — the "dream glow" core
- Highlight: H65° (golden yellow), Sat 22% — assertive golden highlight warmth
- Shadow: H194° (cool teal), Sat 8% — subtle cool shadow complement
- Blending: 50 — balanced transition between shadow warmth and highlight coolness

### What It's NOT
- Not dark/moody — it's luminous and bright
- Not high-contrast — contrast is negative, tonal range is compressed
- Not grain-heavy — no grain at all (clean, smooth aesthetic)
- Not neutral — unmistakably warm golden with intentional color character

### STYLEGUIDE Deviations
This preset was created before STYLEGUIDE conventions. Key deviations (all appear intentional):
- |Vibrance(64) − Saturation(-9)| = 73 — massive gap (STYLEGUIDE max: 5). This is the core aesthetic mechanism — extreme Vibrance on a desaturated base creates the signature "dream glow" midtone pop.
- Temperature/Tint set (5900K, +13) — warm amber WB shift
- No Sharpness attribute (LR defaults to 40 — fine since no grain)
- No grain, so grain protection rules don't apply
- Blue channel curve non-linear (intentional creative curve shaping)

## Best-Suited Images

- Warm-tone lifestyle and editorial
- Backlit scenes with rim lighting
- Flat-light images that need warmth and atmosphere
- Not suited for: neutral/accurate color work, product photography, cool-tone scenes
