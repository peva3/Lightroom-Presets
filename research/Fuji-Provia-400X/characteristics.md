# Fujichrome Provia 400X — Aesthetic Characteristics for Lightroom Presets

## Overview

Fuji Provia 400X (RXP) was a rare and remarkable film — a 400-speed color slide film. Most slide films max out at ISO 100-200, making a 400-speed transparency film a genuine technical achievement. Discontinued around 2013, it remains one of the most sought-after discontinued slide films. This document serves as a reference for creating a Lightroom preset that emulates Provia 400X.

## Core Aesthetic Traits

### 1. High-Speed Slide Performance
The defining feature: ISO 400 in a slide film format. This enabled:
- **Handheld slide photography in lower light** — unprecedented for transparency film
- **Faster shutter speeds** for action/wildlife on slide film
- **Small-aperture depth of field** while maintaining handheld speeds
- **Push processing to 800/1600** with acceptable results (remarkable for E-6)

### 2. Provia Color Palette (with Speed Tradeoffs)
Provia 400X shares the Provia family color philosophy — accurate, moderate, universal — but with expected speed-class compromises:
- **Slightly less saturated than Provia 100F** — the tradeoff for +2 stops of speed
- **Maintained neutral white balance** — same slightly-cool Provia character
- **Good skin tones** — usable for portraits at 400 speed
- **Clean blues** — Provia's characteristic clean-rendered skies

### 3. Moderate Grain (RMS ~11)
More visible grain than Provia 100F (RMS 8) or Astia (RMS 7), but remarkably fine for a 400-speed film. Grain structure is smooth and uniform — not "chunky" like consumer 400-speed films.

### 4. Moderate-High Contrast
Contrast is slightly higher than Provia 100F — a consequence of the faster emulsion. Shadows go to black with conviction, and highlights clip with slide-film abruptness. The ±1/3 stop latitude demands precise metering.

## Comparison: Provia 400X vs. Provia 100F

| Characteristic | Provia 400X | Provia 100F |
|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Speed** | ISO 400 | ISO 100 |
| **Grain** | RMS ~11 (visible but fine) | RMS 8 (nearly invisible) |
| **Saturation** | Moderate (slightly less) | Moderate |
| **Contrast** | Moderate-High | Moderate |
| **Sharpness** | Good | Excellent |
| **Exposure latitude** | ±1/3 stop | ±1/2 stop |
| **Push capability** | Excellent (800/1600) | Limited (200) |
| **Best use** | Low-light, action, wildlife | All-purpose |
