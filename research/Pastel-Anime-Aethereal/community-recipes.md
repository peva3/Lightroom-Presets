## 1. PresetsStore — "Anime Style" Free Lightroom Preset (2021)

**Source:** [presetsstore.com/tutorial/anime-style-free-lightroom-preset](https://presetsstore.com/tutorial/anime-style-free-lightroom-preset)

A step-by-step guide with screenshots. The preset emulates the look of Japanese anime cartoons on real photos.

### Light Panel (Basic Adjustments)

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Contrast | **-42** |
| Highlights | **-100** |
| Shadows | **MAX (+100)** |
| Whites | **+14** |
| Blacks | **+18** |

### Color / HSL Adjustments

- Sky colours pushed toward cyan/teal
- Greens shifted toward mint/blue-green
- Overall saturation moderately boosted

### Effects

- **Clarity:** added (moderate push for "drawn" texture)
- **Split Tone:** applied (highlights toward warm, shadows toward cool — typical anime day-for-night colour-contrast trick)

### Result

- Bright, colourful, high-contrast-lift photo with flattened highlights and opened shadows
- "Anime" sky and greenery from HSL shifts
- Works best on outdoor daylight images with sky, greenery, and buildings

## 3. YouTube — "Pastel Anime Inspired Lightroom Preset Tutorial" (2020)

**Source:** YouTube — search "pastel anime inspired lightroom preset tutorial and free dng" (Sep 25, 2020)

### Reported Settings (community comments / description)

- **Tone Curve:** S-curve with lifted shadow anchor (faded black point)
- **Split Toning:** pastel pink in highlights (~H330–350, S15–25), teal/cyan in shadows (~H190–210, S15–25)
- **HSL:**
  - Blue hue → shifted toward teal (~–10 to –30)
  - Green hue → shifted toward mint (~–20 to –40)
  - Aqua luminance → boosted for sky brightness
- **Texture/Clarity:** slight negative clarity (–10 to –20) for the "soft/dreamy" diffusion

## Summary: The Recurring "Anime Aethereal" Recipe Pattern

Across all community recipes, the consistent pattern for achieving a pastel anime / aethereal look is:

| Adjustment | Direction |
|------------|-----------|
| Contrast | Lowered (–20 to –50) |
| Highlights | Strongly pulled down (–80 to –100) |
| Shadows | Strongly lifted (+80 to +100) |
| Blacks | Lifted (fade the black point) |
| Whites | Slight boost (+10 to +20) |
| Clarity | Slight negative (–10 to –20) for softness |
| Tone Curve | S-curve with lifted shadows |
| HSL — Blue Hue | Shift toward teal (–10 to –30) |
| HSL — Green Hue | Shift toward mint (–20 to –40) |
| HSL — Aqua Luminance | Boosted for sky glow |
| Split Tone — Highlights | Pink/magenta/warm (~H330, S10–25) |
| Split Tone — Shadows | Teal/cyan/cool (~H195, S10–25) |
| Grain | Optional, small amount for texture |
