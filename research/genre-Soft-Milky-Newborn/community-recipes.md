# Soft Milky Newborn Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Soft Milky Newborn Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/NewbornPhotography: overwhelmingly favors high-key, soft, creamy edits. Julia Trotti: negative Clarity + positive Orange Lum for soft skin. Anita Sadowska: negative Clarity brushed on skin areas. cinemorapresets.com: Milk Bath formula uses Contrast -15 to -25, Exposure +0.5 to +0.8.
