# Night Sky Landscape Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Night Sky Landscape Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/LandscapeAstro: foreground blending is the hardest part. Mark Denney night landscape: shadows way up, contrast moderate, dehaze light. lonleyspeck.com night landscape technique: foreground layer + sky layer. cinemorapresets.com: Night Landscape formula uses high shadows, moderate dehaze.
