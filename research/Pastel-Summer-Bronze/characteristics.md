# Pastel Summer Bronze — Lightroom Preset Characteristics

**Creator-tuned preset** — warm, pastel bronze summer aesthetic with airy blue skies, golden skin tones, and a cinematic tone curve. Combines the "pastel" lightness of high-key summer photography with a warm "bronze" undertone.

## Key Characteristics

- **WB**: Warm 6200K / Tint +5 — golden-hour warmth without going orange
- **Contrast**: -10 (soft filmic, not flat)
- **Highlights**: -40 (smooth shoulder rolloff)
- **Shadows**: +25 (lifted for airiness)
- **Tone curve**: Cinematic with lifted blacks (0,15) and soft highlight ceiling (255,240)
- **Vibrance/Saturation gap**: +35/-10 = 45pt — extreme midtone pop on desaturated base
- **Blue Hue**: -45 toward cyan — signature move, very aggressive
- **Blue Luminance**: +40 — bright, airy sky
- **Orange/Yellow Sat**: +20/+15 — golden bronze skin tones
- **Green Sat**: -20 — suppress foliage
- **Color Grade**: Warm amber midtones+highlights (H45/S10-15), cool teal shadows (H210/S5), Blending 75
- **Calibration**: Red H+10/S+5, Blue H-20/S+5 — shifts primaries for bronze richness
- **Grain**: 15/35/50 — subtle, not aggressive
- **Sharpness**: 15, LuminanceSmoothing: 15
- **Group**: Creative Cinematic
