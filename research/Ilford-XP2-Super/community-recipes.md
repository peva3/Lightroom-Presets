## t3mujinpack Reference

**Source**: t3mujinpack — community B&W film emulation pack

XP2 Super is referenced as a unique chromogenic B&W film with these defining characteristics:
- C-41 process dye-cloud image — essentially grainless
- Softer contrast than silver B&W films
- Very wide exposure latitude (EI 50-800)
- Very fine grain — near-zero at normal viewing
- Grain: Amount 10, Size 15, Roughness 45

## Community Core Settings for XP2 Super Look

### Basic Panel

XP2 Super has soft contrast, smooth tonality, and essentially zero grain:

| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Contrast | -5 to +5 | Softer than silver B&W — very gentle |
| Highlights | -35 to -45 | Wide highlight latitude |
| Shadows | +20 to +30 | Open, lifted shadows |
| Whites | -5 to +5 | Near-neutral whites |
| Blacks | -5 to -10 | Gentle black point, not deep |
