# Deep Forest Green — Community Recipes

## Source
Cinemora Presets: https://cinemorapresets.com/forest-lightroom-presets

## Published Slider Values
Exposure: -0.05 | Contrast: +18 | Highlights: -30 | Shadows: +15 | Whites: +8 | Blacks: -12 | Green H-15/S-25/L-10

## Description
Deep Forest Green preset. Uses targeted HSL adjustments on greens to create rich, deep forest tones with green shadow color grading. Green hue shifted toward yellow-olive (H-15) for natural woodland tones. Reduced green saturation (S-25) prevents digital neon-green. Darker green luminance (L-10) adds density and depth. Green shadow color grading reinforces the forest atmosphere.
