# Deep Forest Green — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Deep, rich forest green tones
- Green hue toward yellow-olive for natural woodland
- Reduced green saturation prevents digital neon
- Darker green luminance adds density
- Green-tinted shadows reinforce forest atmosphere

## Usage
Best for: Forest photography, nature landscapes, woodland scenes, any image where deep, rich greens should dominate the palette.
