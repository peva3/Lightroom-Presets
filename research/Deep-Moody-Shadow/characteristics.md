# Deep Moody Shadow — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Dark and brooding
- Significantly desaturated (-18 Sat)
- Cool blue shadow tones for depth
- Heavy vignette for focal centering
- Strong highlight control to maintain mood

## Usage
Best for: Dramatic portraits, moody landscapes, fine art photography, any scene where darkness and atmosphere are primary.
