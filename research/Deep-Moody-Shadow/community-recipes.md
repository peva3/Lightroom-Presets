Deep Moody Shadow preset. The most aggressively desaturated preset in the collection with heavy vignette. Creates a dark, brooding look with strong cool blue shadow color grading. Significantly desaturated for a near-monochromatic feel with cool blue undertones. Heavy vignette draws focus to the center. Strong highlight recovery prevents bright spots from breaking the moody atmosphere.
