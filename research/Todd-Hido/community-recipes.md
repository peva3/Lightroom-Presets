# Community Recipes — Todd Hido

## Known Approaches

### From r/photography and r/nightphotography
- Hido's night window aesthetic is widely imitated on social media
- Through-windshield shooting at night is the #1 discussed technique
- "Hido blue" has become a recognizable color palette term

### From YouTube Tutorials
- North Borders (500K+): Moody night editing with cool blue/magenta casts
- 7th Era (400K+): Night photography grading that overlaps significantly with Hido aesthetics
- Common technical notes: underexpose in-camera, lift shadows in post, cool color balance

### From Fine Art Photography Communities
- Hido's color palette is sodium vapor streetlights (warm) mixed with blue hour ambient
- The magenta shift in shadows comes from mixed lighting (fluorescent + sodium + moonlight)
- "House Hunting" is the most referenced body of work for color reference

### Community Consensus
- Blue/magenta shadow cast is the signature
- Underexposure with shadow lift creates the night paradox
- Negative dehaze for atmospheric fog/mist
- Desaturation of greens (night lawns aren't green)
- Works best on images with artificial light sources in a dark frame
