# Color Decisive Moment — Characteristics

## Use Case
Color street photography — decisive moment, urban color, street portraiture. Quick processing for fast turnaround. Works on diverse urban color palettes.

## Key Techniques
Mild Exposure (+0.10) + moderate Contrast (+15) for quick tonal correction without over-processing. Green desaturation (-20) and hue shift (+10) tames urban foliage. Blue Sat -10 calms sky for street-level focus. Orange Lum +5 for slight skin brightness. Clarity +8 for urban texture (concrete, brick, asphalt). Subtle cool/warm split. |Vibrance−Saturation|=0. 17 attributes — clean, fast, repeatable.
