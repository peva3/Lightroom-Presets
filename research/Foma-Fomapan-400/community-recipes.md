# Foma Fomapan 400 — Community Recipes

## Community Notes (from forums)
- No dedicated Fuji X Weekly recipe exists for Fomapan 400
- Photrio: "The poor man's Tri-X" — similar character at half the price
- Fomapan 400 @ EI 800 in Rodinal is a celebrated budget push combination
- Reddit r/analog: extensive discussion of Fomapan 400's "character" vs "quality control"
- Compared to Kentmere Pan 400 — both are budget 400 films, Fomapan has more "personality"
- Forum consensus: best results at box speed in D-76 or XTOL; Rodinal for dramatic effect

## Massive Dev Chart Data
- D-76 stock: 8 min at 20°C
- Rodinal 1+50: 10 min at 20°C (classic dramatic combination)
- XTOL stock: 8 min at 20°C (smoothest results)
- HC-110 Dilution B: 6 min at 20°C
- Push to 800: add ~40% development time
- Push to 1600: add ~70% time, expect heavy grain and contrast

## LR Preset Design Notes
- Pronounced cubic grain: GrainAmount=45, Size=35, Roughness=60
  - More grain than premium 400 films (HP5+, Delta 400)
  - Less grain than Delta 3200 or pushed Tri-X
- Moderate-high contrast (+25) — punchier than most 400 films
- Standard panchromatic GrayMixer: Red +15, Orange +12, Blue -30
  - Reduced red (Czech spectral sensitivity)
  - Stronger blue darkening for dramatic skies
- Deep blacks (-28) — Fomapan's characteristic rich dark tones
- Moderate highlight reduction (-20)
- Modest shadow lift (+15)
- All STYLEGUIDE rules: Sharpness=10 with grain, Clarity/Texture/Dehaze=0, no Calibration, no WB
- The deep blacks and pronounced grain are the defining characteristics
