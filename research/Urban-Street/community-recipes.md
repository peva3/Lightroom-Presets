## Description
Urban Street preset for street photography. High clarity and moderate contrast with aggressive highlight recovery for harsh sunlight conditions. Designed for the grit, contrast, and energy of urban life — sharp textures in concrete and brick, controlled highlights for bright sky patches, and slightly elevated exposure for shadow visibility.
