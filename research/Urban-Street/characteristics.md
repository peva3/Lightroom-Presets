# Urban Street — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Sharp, punchy street photography
- High clarity for concrete/brick/urban material texture
- Strong highlight recovery for harsh sunlight
- Slightly elevated exposure for shadow visibility
- Cool shadow tint against neutral highlights

## Usage
Best for: Street photography, urban exploration, architecture, documentary work, and any scene with strong textures and mixed lighting.
