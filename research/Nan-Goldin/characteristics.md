# Nan Goldin

## Biography
- **Name**: Nan Goldin (born 1953)
- **Nationality**: American
- **Era**: 1970s–present

## Philosophy
- "My work is about my tribe. It's about the people I'm close to."
- Photography as visual diary — intensely personal, autobiographical
- Documented the downtown NYC scene: drag queens, drug culture, AIDS crisis, friends, lovers
- "The Ballad of Sexual Dependency" — her defining body of work, named after a Brecht play
- "I used to think I could never lose anyone if I photographed them enough."

## Technical Approach
- **Gear**: 35mm camera, often a Nikon FM2, later Leica, Hasselblad
- **Film**: Kodak color negative (various), often pushed
- **Flash**: Direct, on-camera flash — the defining technique
- **Lighting**: No modifiers, no studio, no assistants — the flash as bare bulb
- **Processing**: Drugstore/C-41 processing; no special treatment

## Visual Signature
- **Direct flash aesthetic**: Hard shadows behind subjects, red-eye, blown highlights on faces — the snapshot turned art
- **Warm color cast**: The flash balanced for daylight creates warmth; mixed ambient/flash creates color temperature clashes
- **Slight underexposure**: Flash falloff means backgrounds go dark, subjects pop against deep shadows
- **Intimate proximity**: The camera is in the room, on the bed, at the table — there is no distance
- **C-41 snapshot colors**: Drugstore processing gave uneven color — cyan/green in some frames, warm/magenta in others — a chaotic, alive color palette
- **Grain structure**: 35mm color negative has visible grain, especially pushed — it adds to the raw intimacy

## Iconic Images
- *Nan and Brian in Bed* (1983)
- *Nan One Month After Being Battered* (1984)
- *The Ballad of Sexual Dependency* (1986 book)

## Preset Design Notes
- Warm flash color via highlight color grading (Hue 35, Sat 15)
- Slight underexposure as base (-0.25 EV)
- Warm shadow cast for overall warmth (flash signature)
- Red and orange saturation boosted (skin warmth under flash)
- Moderate grain for 35mm C-41 nostalgia
- Grain protection rules applied (Sharpness 10, Clarity 0, Texture 0, Dehaze 0)
- Documentary Blending at 50
