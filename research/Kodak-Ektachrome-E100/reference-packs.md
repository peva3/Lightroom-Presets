# Reference Packs — Ektachrome E100 Presets & Profiles

This document catalogs commercial and free preset packs that include Kodak Ektachrome E100 emulations, either as standalone presets or as part of larger film simulation collections.

---

## Commercial Packs

### RNI (Really Nice Images) — All Films 5
- **Product:** RNI All Films 5 for Adobe Lightroom & Camera Raw
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — "Kodak Ektachrome E100" profile (both standard and faded variants)
- **Type:** Camera profiles (.dcp) + develop presets
- **Notes:** RNI includes E100 in their "Slide Film" category alongside Fuji Provia, Velvia, and Astia. RNI profiles are widely regarded as the most accurate colorimetric film emulations available. E100 profile is neutral-cool with excellent skin tone handling.
- **Price:** ~$99 (full bundle)

### VSCO Film (Discontinued but Widely Circulated)
- **Product:** VSCO Film 01 (Modern), possibly Film 02
- **E100 Inclusion:** No direct E100 preset in original packs 01–07. However, VSCO's "Kodak E100G" and "Kodak E100VS" are included in VSCO Film 02 (Classic) and provide the closest relatives.
- **Type:** Develop presets + camera profiles
- **Notes:** VSCO Film is discontinued (2024) but remains widely installed. The E100G preset is a good starting point for E100 emulation — E100 is essentially a modernized E100G.

### The Archetype Process (TAP)
- **Product:** TAP Kodak Pro Pack / The Slide Film Pack
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — specifically profiled for multiple digital cameras
- **Type:** Camera-matching profiles (.dcp) for Lightroom/Capture One
- **Notes:** TAP profiles are built per-camera-sensor, which provides more accurate emulation than generic presets. The E100 profile is based on actual film scans matched to digital sensor response.
- **Price:** ~$75–125 per pack

### Mastin Labs
- **Product:** Kodak Original Pack (originally), now adventure/portrait packs
- **E100 Inclusion:** No dedicated E100 preset, but their "Ektachrome" style is embedded in several packs. The original "Kodak Everyday" pack referenced Ektachrome.
- **Type:** Develop presets
- **Notes:** Mastin Labs presets are less colorimetrically precise but are popular for their film-inspired tones and workflow. They use profile-based adjustments.

### DxO FilmPack
- **Product:** DxO FilmPack 7
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — "Kodak Ektachrome 100" rendering
- **Type:** Built-in film rendering engine
- **Notes:** DxO's version is based on physical film characterization. Includes grain simulation, color rendering, and contrast curves derived from actual film measurements.
- **Price:** ~$79–129

### Nik Collection — Analog Efex
- **Product:** Nik Collection Analog Efex Pro
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — Ektachrome E100 in the "Camera Kit" category
- **Type:** Plugin-based film simulation
- **Notes:** Analog Efex provides multiple Ektachrome variants including clean, vintage, and cross-processed looks.

### Dehancer
- **Product:** Dehancer Film Plugin (Lightroom/Photoshop/DaVinci Resolve)
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — "Kodak Ektachrome E100" with grain, halation, and bloom controls
- **Type:** Plugin with film profiles
- **Notes:** Dehancer uses a spectral model for film emulation. Includes E100 in multiple variants. Highly regarded for video color grading, also works for stills.
- **Price:** ~$199 (one-time) or subscription

### Cobalt Image
- **Product:** Cobalt Film Profiles — Kodak Slide Collection
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — "Kodak E100" profile
- **Type:** Camera-matching DCP profiles
- **Notes:** Cobalt makes profiles for specific camera models. Their E100 profile is based on direct film-to-digital profiling methodology.
- **Price:** ~$35 per profile or bundles available

### FilmConvert / CineMatch
- **Product:** FilmConvert Nitrate (video) / CineMatch
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — includes Ektachrome E100 as a film stock option
- **Type:** Video color grading plugin with camera-specific sensor data
- **Notes:** Primarily video-focused. CineMatch maps your camera sensor to film stocks including E100.

### Presetpro
- **Product:** Film Emulation Presets Collection
- **E100 Inclusion:** Yes — "Kodak Ektachrome E100" preset
- **Type:** Develop presets
- **Notes:** Mid-tier preset pack. Less precise than RNI or Cobalt, but substantially cheaper.

## Free / Community Packs

### ReallyNiceGuy (GitHub / Free)
- Various community-developed .xmp presets floating on GitHub, Reddit, and film forums
- Quality varies greatly; most are tone curve + HSL recipes rather than proper profiles
- Search terms: "Ektachrome Lightroom preset GitHub" or "E100 .xmp"

### PresetLove / FilterGrade Freebies
- Occasional free "Ektachrome" preset downloads
- Typically basic contrast + color shifts; not camera-profiled

### Matt Day (YouTube)
- Has shared approximate settings in video descriptions and on his blog
- See community-recipes.md "Recipe B" for details

## Comparison: E100 vs E100G vs E100VS in Preset Packs

| Film | VSCO | RNI | Cobalt | DxO | TAP |
|------|------|-----|--------|-----|-----|
| E100 (2018) | — | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| E100G (vintage) | Yes (02) | Yes | — | — | — |
| E100VS (vintage) | Yes (02) | Yes | — | — | — |

VSCO's E100G is notably warmer and slightly less contrasty than the modern E100, while VSCO's E100VS is significantly more saturated with pushed reds and yellows.

## Summary

The most accurate Ektachrome E100 digital emulations are found in:
1. **RNI All Films 5** — best overall accuracy, widely available
2. **Cobalt Image** — best per-camera profiling
3. **The Archetype Process** — excellent for specific camera models
4. **DxO FilmPack** — best integrated workflow

For those seeking free options, community .xmp recipes (documented in community-recipes.md) are the most accessible starting point, though less consistent across different camera sensors.
