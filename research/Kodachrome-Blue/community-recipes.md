## Description
Based on improperly color-corrected Kodachrome scans — Kodachrome (a B&W film with dyes added in K-14 development) is notoriously difficult to scan, often producing a pronounced blue cast. This Recipe embraces that scanning artifact, emphasizing the legendary "Kodachrome blue" sky signature. Deep cyan-blue skies, suppressed warm tones, cool overall balance. The National Geographic mid-century color palette variant.

Not intended as an everyday Recipe — use Kodachrome 64 or Kodachrome II for general photography.
