# Ricoh Superior 100 — Lightroom Preset Characteristics

Ricoh Superior 100 is a vintage color negative film from Ricoh, offering warm color reproduction with moderate contrast and a slightly muted green channel.

## Key Aesthetic Traits
- Warm temperature (6000K) with green tint offset (-10)
- Moderate contrast (+15) with strong highlight compression (-50)
- Lifted shadows (+40) for film-like shadow detail
- Warm amber midtone/highlight color grade (H45/S15-20)
- Green shadows (H130/S5)
- Slightly reduced clarity (-5)
- Cinematic S-curve (0,10/45,35/128,128/192,196/255,245)
