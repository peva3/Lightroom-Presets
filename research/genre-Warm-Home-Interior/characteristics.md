# Warm Home Interior Lightroom Preset — Characteristics

## Use Case
The Warm Home Interior Lightroom preset recreates this look digitally using Lightroom's color grading tools for authentic film emulation. Cozy residential interiors — living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens with warm decor. Enhancing wood tones and natural warmth.

## Key Techniques
Warm shadow split (H35 S10) + warm highlight (H45 S8) create inviting glow. Orange/Red/Yellow saturation boosts enhance wood, leather, and warm textiles. Yellow hue -10 shifts toward orange for golden wood tones. |Vibrance−Saturation|=0 (safe). Orange Lum +10 brightens without oversaturating. Just 17 attributes.

## Safety Check
- ProcessVersion: 15.4
- Treatment: Color
- Look Profile: Adobe Color
- Curve: Cinematic (0,20 / 64,55 / 128,128 / 192,196 / 255,235)
- ColorGradeBlending: 75
- Calibration: None (as required)
- Temperature/Tint: None (as required)
- Vibrance−Saturation gap: Checked — within ±5
- HSL Sat max: Within ±60
- Blacks floor: ≥ -30
- Grain protection: N/A (no grain)
