# Warm Home Interior Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Warm Home Interior Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/RealEstatePhotography: warm edits preferred for residential (vs cool for modern commercial). Golden-hour style interior photography is trending on Instagram. cinemorapresets.com: Cozy Home formula uses warm split toning, wood enhancement.
