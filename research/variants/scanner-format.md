# Scanner & Format Variants

## Scanner Variants (3)
Scanner-specific color biases:
- Noritsu: Warmer, more contrasty, more saturated. ColorGradeBalance +10, Highlights warmer +5 hue.
- Frontier: Cooler, cyan-leaning shadows, less contrast. Shadow hue +15 cooler, Balance -10.

Presets:
- Kodak Gold 200 Noritsu, Kodak Gold 200 Frontier, Kodak Portra 400 Noritsu

## Format Variants (3)
Larger formats = finer grain, smoother tonality:
- 120 (medium format): Grain Amount -10, Size -8, Contrast -3, Shadows +5
- 4x5 (large format): Grain Amount -18, Size -12, Contrast -5, Shadows +8
Min grain floor: Amount >= 3, Size >= 8.

Presets:
- Kodak Portra 400 120, Kodak Portra 400 4x5, Kodak Tri-X 400 120
