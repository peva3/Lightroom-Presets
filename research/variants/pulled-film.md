# Pulled Film Variants (-1 Stop)

## Methodology
Pulling film = overexposing + underdeveloping. Simulated by:
- Exposure +0.30
- Contrast -15
- Highlights recovered less (lifted +15)
- Shadows lifted +15
- Blacks lifted +10
- Grain reduced: Amount -12, Size -8
- Saturation reduced -5

## Presets (4)
- Kodak Portra 400 -1, Portra 800 -1
- Kodak Tri-X 400 -1, Ilford HP5 Plus -1
Produces creamier tonality, finer grain, lower contrast — the "overexposed Portra" look.
