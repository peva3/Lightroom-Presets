# Developer Variants (B&W)

## Methodology
Different developers produce distinct grain structure and contrast characteristics:
- Rodinal: high acutance, prominent grain (+15 Amount, +8 Size, +15 Roughness, +10 Contrast)
- HC-110: moderate grain, good tonal range (+5 Amount, +3 Size, -5 Contrast)
- DD-X: fine grain, full speed (-10 Amount, -8 Size, -5 Contrast)
- Stand Dev: compensating (reduced contrast -15, lifted shadows +15, highlights +10)
- Microphen: maximum speed push (+10 Amount, +8 Size, +8 Contrast)

## Presets (5)
- Kodak Tri-X 400 Rodinal — classic grain monster
- Kodak Tri-X 400 HC-110 — versatile standard
- Ilford HP5 Plus DD-X — clean & sharp
- Ilford HP5 Plus Stand Dev — compensating, edge effects
- Ilford Delta 3200 Microphen — speed-maximized push
