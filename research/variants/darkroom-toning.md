# Darkroom Toning Presets

## Methodology
B&W darkroom toning emulated via ColorGrade wheels on Monochrome treatment:
- Sepia: warm brown (H35/S18 highlights, H25/S12 shadows, Bal +25)
- Selenium: cool purple-brown (H275/S12 highlights, H250/S8 shadows, Bal -10)
- Gold: blue-black (H230/S10 highlights, H220/S8 shadows, Bal -5)
- Copper: reddish-brown (H20/S15 highlights, H15/S12 shadows, Bal +20)
- Split Warm-Cool: warm highlights + cool shadows (H45/H220, Bal 0)

## Presets (5)
- Sepia Tone, Selenium Tone, Gold Tone, Copper Tone, Split Warm-Cool
Based on HP5-like neutral contrast + grain base. Grain: Amount 35, Size 25, Roughness 50.
