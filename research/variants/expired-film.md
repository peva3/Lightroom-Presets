# Expired Film Variants

## Methodology
Expired film develops base fog, loses contrast, and shifts color (typically toward magenta/green).
Simulated by:
- Contrast -12 to -18 (more for 30yr)
- Blacks lifted +12 to +18
- Whites pulled down
- ColorGradeBalance shifted toward warmth (+15-22)
- Shadow hue shifted green/magenta
- Global saturation reduced
- Heavy grain increase: Amount +22-33, Size +12-18, Roughness +18-27
- Green and blue saturation reduced (faded channels)

## Presets (5)
- Kodak Gold 200 Expired 10yr — moderate fade
- Kodak Gold 200 Expired 30yr — extreme fade, heavy fog
- Kodak Portra 400 Expired — magenta cast, grain bloom
- Kodak Tri-X 400 Expired — base fog, softened contrast
- Ilford HP5 Plus Expired — lifted shadows, muted tonality
