# Pushed Film Variants

## Methodology
Pushing film = underexposing (rating higher ISO) + overdeveloping. Simulated by:
- Exposure -0.15 to -0.35
- Contrast +15 to +28
- Highlights pulled down harder
- Shadows made darker
- Blacks decreased (capped at -30 per STYLEGUIDE)
- Grain Amount +15-28, Size +8-15, Roughness +10-20
- Saturation +5-10
