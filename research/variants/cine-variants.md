# Cine Film Variants

## Methodology
Vision3 motion picture film adapted for stills:
- ECN-2 process: flatter contrast (remjet removal + lower gamma), additive grain
- 500T: Tungsten-balanced, faster, cooler cast, higher contrast than 250D

## Presets (2)
- Kodak Vision3 250D ECN-2 — daylight cine neg with ECN-2 process characteristics
- Kodak Vision3 500T ECN-2 — tungsten cine neg, cooler balance, more blue sensitivity

Parent: Kodak Vision3 250D. ECN-2 mods: Contrast -3, Grain +3. 500T mods: Grain +10, Contrast +5, Cooler balance.
