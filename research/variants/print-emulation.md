# Print Emulation Presets

## Methodology
Digital emulation of darkroom print processes:
- RA-4: Color negative → RA-4 paper (slight warmth, lifted blacks, print contrast)
- Ilford MG Warmtone: B&W → warm-tone fiber paper (H40/35 warm split, softer grain)
- C-Print: Digital → chromogenic color print (more pop, higher saturation, clean)

## Presets (3)
- RA-4 Color Print — standard color darkroom print look
- Ilford MG Warmtone — warm-toned B&W fiber paper
- C-Print — digital-to-chromogenic print pipeline
