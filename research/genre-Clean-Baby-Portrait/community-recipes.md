# Clean Baby Portrait Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Clean Baby Portrait Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/BabyPhotography consensus: parents want natural, clean, 'what my baby really looks like' results. Jessica Kobeissi portrait base: Orange Lum +10, Orange Sat -10, low global saturation. cinemorapresets.com: Clean Portrait formula uses minimal adjustments.

- Fixed: Follows Genre defaults (cinematic curve, Blacks=0, Blending=75)
- Hard bans clean: No Calibration, no Temperature/Tint, HSL Sat all within ±60
- Boilerplate verified: ProcessVersion=15.4, Treatment=Color, Adobe Color Look, 75 Blending
- Grain rule: No grain — standard detail settings apply
