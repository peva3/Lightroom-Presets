# Kodak Ektachrome 400X — Aesthetic Characteristics for Lightroom Presets

## The Film

Kodak Ektachrome 400X (E-6, daylight balanced at 5500K) was Kodak's high-speed professional slide film. At ISO 400, it was the fastest Ektachrome in the lineup, designed for sports, low-light, and situations where shutter speed was critical. It represented the practical upper limit of E-6 slide technology. This document serves as a reference for creating a Lightroom preset that emulates Ektachrome 400X.

## Core Aesthetic

### 1. High-Speed Slide Character
- **High contrast** — aggressive curve to compensate for the speed, creating punchy projected images
- **Warm-biased neutrality** — warmer than E100/E200, a characteristic of the 400-speed emulsion
- **Pushed saturation** — the high-speed emulsion required more aggressive dye couplers, resulting in more saturated rendering than slower Ektachromes
- **Visible grain structure** — RMS ~14-15, the most noticeable grain in the Ektachrome family

### 2. Color Palette by Hue

| Hue | Ektachrome 400X Rendering |
|-----|---------------------------|
| **Blue (sky)** | Deep saturated blue, slightly warm |
| **Blue (shadow)** | Neutral to slightly warm (less cool than E100) |
| **Red** | Punchy, saturated red — more aggressive than E100 |
| **Green (foliage)** | Vibrant yellow-green |
| **Skin tones** | Warm and saturated — can be unflattering on fair skin |
| **Yellow/Orange** | Intensely warm, golden |
| **Whites** | Clean, slightly warm |

### 3. Contrast & Dynamic Range
- **High contrast** — punchy, aggressive slide film look
- **RMS ~14-15** — noticeable grain, especially in midtones and skies
- **Dynamic range ~6-7 stops** — slightly narrower than slower slide films
- Pronounced highlight clipping and deep shadow crush

### 4. The "Action Slide" Identity
Ektachrome 400X was marketed for:
- Sports and action photography (where high shutter speeds matter)
- Wildlife (telephoto lenses need speed)
- Indoor events with available light
- Journalism where pushing film was common

The aesthetic trades refinement for impact — it's a bold, punchy, high-energy look.

## Comparison: Ektachrome 400X vs E100

| Attribute | Ektachrome 400X | E100 (2018) |
|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| Contrast | High | High |
| Saturation | Higher | Moderate-high |
| Warmth | Noticeably warm | Cool-leaning |
| Skin tones | Warm, saturated | Cool, pale |
| Grain | RMS ~14-15 (visible) | RMS 8 (near-invisible) |
| Dynamic range | ~6-7 stops | ~7-8 stops |
| Overall vibe | Punchy action slide | Clean modern slide |
