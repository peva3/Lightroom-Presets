# Detail Pop Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Detail Pop Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/WildlifePhotography: detail is everything. Pro wildlife photographers use Clarity +15-25 on subjects, negative on backgrounds. cinemorapresets.com: Wildlife Detail formula uses Texture + Clarity combo, green naturalization, selective warm saturation.

- Fixed: Follows Genre defaults (cinematic curve, Blacks=0, Blending=75)
- Hard bans clean: No Calibration, no Temperature/Tint, HSL Sat all within ±60
- Boilerplate verified: ProcessVersion=15.4, Treatment=Color, Adobe Color Look, 75 Blending
