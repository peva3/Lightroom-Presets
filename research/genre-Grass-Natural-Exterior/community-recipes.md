# Grass Natural Exterior Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Grass Natural Exterior Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/LandscapePhotography common practice: Green Sat -10 to -20, Green Hue +15 to +25. Mark Denney: 'digital greens are the enemy.' cinemorapresets.com: Landscape Natural formula uses green desaturation + hue shift.
