# Classic Portrait Glow — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Soft, glowing portraits
- Negative texture smooths skin
- Negative clarity adds gentle bloom
- Mild contrast maintains natural definition
- Warm, flattering skin tone rendering
- Subtle golden highlights

## Usage
Best for: Portrait photography, wedding photography, headshots, any people-focused work where skin softening and glow are desired.
