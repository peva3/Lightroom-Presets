## Published Slider Values

## Description
Classic Portrait Glow preset. The only preset in the collection using negative Texture and Clarity for skin softening. Creates a gentle, flattering portrait look. Negative texture smooths skin without losing overall sharpness. Negative clarity adds gentle bloom for a classic portrait glow. Mild contrast maintains definition. Slight saturation boost for healthy skin tones. Warm shadow tones with golden highlights for flattering skin rendering.
