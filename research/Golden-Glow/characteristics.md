# Golden Glow — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Golden hour enhancement
- Strong warm temperature + tint for rich golden light
- Warm color grading in shadows, midtones, and highlights
- Orange luminance boost for glowing skin tones
- Slightly elevated exposure for bright, airy golden feel
- Subtle negative clarity for golden bloom

## Usage
Best for: Golden hour portraits, sunset landscapes, warm lifestyle photography, any backlit warm-light scene.
