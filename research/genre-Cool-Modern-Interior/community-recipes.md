# Cool Modern Interior Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Cool Modern Interior Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. Modern architecture photographers favor cool, crisp tones. r/ArchitecturePhotography: slight blue split preferred. cinemorapresets.com: Modern Minimal formula uses cool shadows, negative saturation, edge clarity. North Borders: cool tones for urban/architectural work.
