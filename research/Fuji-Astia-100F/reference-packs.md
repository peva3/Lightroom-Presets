# Paid Preset Packs That Include Fuji Astia 100F

## Major Commercial Film Emulation Packs

### 1. VSCO Film (discontinued, but profiles still usable)

VSCO released Astia 100F profiles across multiple packs:

| Pack | Astia Variants Included |
|------|------------------------|
| **VSCO Film 01** (Modern Films) | Likely included given the era |
| **VSCO Film 02** (Classic Films) | Not included |
| **VSCO Film 05** (Archetype Films) | May include as part of slide film collection |

**Status:** VSCO discontinued their desktop Film presets in 2019. Existing purchasers can still download and use them. The profiles (.dcp) work in current Lightroom Classic.

**Quality assessment:** VSCO's Astia profiles had a good reputation for color accuracy but their default contrast was often too high — users typically needed to reduce the preset strength or manually lower contrast.

### 2. RNI All Films (Really Nice Images)

RNI offers Astia in their film simulation packages:

| Product | Status |
|---------|--------|
| **RNI All Films 5 Pro** | Includes Astia 100F |
| **RNI All Films 5 — Slide Film Pack** | Likely includes Astia |
| **RNI Films (iOS app)** | Astia available in-app |

**Format:** Lightroom profiles (.xmp) and camera profiles (.dcp)

**Quality assessment:** RNI is generally considered one of the better film simulation makers. Their Astia is known for:
- Good highlight roll-off
- Natural skin tones
- Moderate contrast (closer to the real film than VSCO)

## Free / Open-Source Options

### Fujifilm X RAW Studio / In-Camera
- If you own a Fujifilm X-series or GFX camera, the **"Astia/Soft" film simulation** is built into the JPEG engine
- Can be applied to RAW files via Fujifilm X RAW Studio (free desktop software)
- Not a Lightroom preset, but a reference for what Fuji considers the "Astia look"

### RawTherapee / Darktable
- Open-source RAW processors
- Community-created film simulation presets sometimes include Astia
- Search community forums for "Astia" HaldCLUT files

### G'MIC (GIMP plugin)
- Includes some film simulation filters
- Unlikely to have a dedicated Astia filter

## Summits / Independent Creators

Several individual preset creators on platforms like Etsy, Gumroad, and Creative Market sell Astia-inspired Lightroom presets. Quality varies widely. Common names:

- "Astia Portrait" presets
- "Fuji Slide Film" bundles
- "Soft Transparency" presets (evocative name referencing Astia)

**Note:** These are typically color-graded approximations, not measured film profiles. Buyer beware.

## Fujifilm's Own Digital Implementation

The **"Astia/Soft" film simulation mode** in Fujifilm X-series and GFX cameras (introduced ~2011) is Fujifilm's official digital interpretation. Key parameters:
- Soft highlight roll-off
- Natural, slightly subdued saturation
- Skin-friendly color response
- Lower overall contrast than Provia/Standard or Velvia/Vivid modes

This simulation is available:
- In-camera (JPEG and as a basis for RAW processing)
- Via Fujifilm X RAW Studio
- Via Capture One (Fujifilm film simulations are included for Fuji RAF files)
- Via Lightroom (Adobe Camera Matching profiles include Fuji sims for supported cameras)

**Important:** The in-camera "Astia/Soft" sim is NOT the same as a Lightroom preset — it only works with Fujifilm RAF files.

## Recommendation for Lightroom Users

For the most accurate Astia 100F emulation in Lightroom:

1. **RNI All Films 5 Pro** (most accessible, still actively sold)
2. **Cobalt Fujichrome Pack** (highly regarded for color accuracy)
3. **DxO FilmPack 7** (good all-around film emulation suite, includes Astia)
4. **Dehancer** (most complete film simulation with grain, halation, bloom; overkill for simple preset needs but the most filmic result)
