# Epic Landscape Lightroom Preset — Community Recipes

## Sources
The Epic Landscape Lightroom preset community recipes compile settings from photographers worldwide. r/LandscapePhotography epic style: vibrant, contrasty, dramatic skies. Peter McKinnon landscape formula: blue sat up, dehaze moderate, clarity moderate. Mark Denney landscape trio: Clarity + Texture + Dehaze in small stacked amounts. cinemorapresets.com: Epic Landscape formula uses strong contrast, saturated blues, green naturalization.
