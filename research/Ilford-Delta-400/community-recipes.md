## t3mujinpack Reference

**Source**: t3mujinpack — community B&W film emulation pack

Delta 400 is referenced with these defining characteristics:
- T-grain modern look — cleaner than HP5+
- Modern rendering, linear tonal scale
- Finer grain than traditional 400-speed films
- Grain: Amount 30, Size 30, Roughness 55

## Community Core Settings for Delta 400 Look

### Basic Panel

Delta 400 is a modern 400-speed film with clean rendering:

| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Contrast | +20 to +30 | Modern, clean contrast |
| Highlights | -30 to -40 | Good highlight protection |
| Shadows | +15 to +25 | Open shadows |
| Whites | +5 to +15 | Clean whites |
| Blacks | -15 to -25 | Defined blacks |

### B&W Mix

Delta 400 has modern panchromatic sensitivity with clean rendering:

| Channel | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Red | +10 to +20 | Natural skin lift |
| Orange | +5 to +15 | Clean skin tones |
| Yellow | 0 to +10 | Subtle highlight |
| Green | -10 to 0 | Slight foliage darkening |
| Aqua | -5 to -10 | Cool tone definition |
| Blue | -15 to -25 | Moderate sky darkening |
| Purple | 0 to -5 | Subtle contrast |
| Magenta | 0 | Neutral |
