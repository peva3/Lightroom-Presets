## Published Slider Values

## Description
Arctic Cinematic / Glacier Blue preset. Creates a cold, icy cinematic look with cool blue color grading and moderate contrast. The negative temperature shift is the defining characteristic — it pushes the image toward cool cyan-blue tones throughout. Designed for winter landscapes, foggy woods, cold outdoor portraits, and modern architecture.

The preset isolates skin tone oranges to keep subjects warm against cold backgrounds. Deep frosty shadow cyans with clean, cool white highlights. Desaturates conflicting yellows and greens to maintain the icy aesthetic.
