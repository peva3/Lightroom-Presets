# Autumn Gold — Characteristics

## Key Aesthetic
- Classic autumn color palette
- Strong green desaturation removes summer tones
- Orange saturation boost enhances fall colors
- Warm temperature shift for golden autumn light
- Yellow shifted toward orange for full fall transformation
- Warm color grading throughout all tonal ranges

## Usage
Best for: Autumn landscapes, fall foliage, seasonal photography, any scene where golden autumn colors should dominate.
