# Eterna Summer — Community Recipes

## Source
Fuji X Weekly: https://fujixweekly.com/2024/04/08/eterna-summer-fujifilm-x-trans-iv-x-trans-v-film-simulation-recipe/

## Fuji X Weekly Recipe
- Film Simulation: Eterna
- Dynamic Range: DR200
- Grain Effect: Strong, Small
- Color Chrome Effect: Strong
- Color Chrome FX Blue: Strong (X-Trans IV) / Weak (X-Trans V)
- White Balance: Daylight, +3 Red & -7 Blue
- Highlight: +2.5
- Shadow: 0
- Color: +4
- Sharpness: -1
- High ISO NR: -4
- Clarity: -3
- ISO: Auto, up to ISO 6400
- Exposure Compensation: -1/3 to +2/3 (typically)

## Description
A warm, golden cinematic look built on Fujifilm's Eterna film simulation. Not modeled after any specific film but designed to sit alongside Classic Chrome Recipes while leveraging Eterna's unique cinematic tonality. Eterna has a "cinematic" tonality that Classic Chrome doesn't provide — lower contrast, smoother tonal transitions, and a more film-like rendering.

The Daylight WB with warm shift (+3 Red / -7 Blue) creates golden-hour-like warmth even in midday sun. Negative clarity adds atmospheric bloom. Designed for sunny daylight photography delivering warm summertime vibes.
