## Background
Co-created by Anders Lindborg and John Sevigny, calibrated against Steve McCurry's final roll of Kodachrome 64, developed July 12, 2010 at Dwayne's Photo in Parsons, Kansas. Unlike Ritchie Roesch's Kodachrome 64 Recipe (calibrated to projected slides), this Recipe targets professionally-scanned Kodachrome as rendered by Richard Jackson.

## Description
Afghan Girl warmth. Deep, saturated reds. Slightly underexposed (-1/3 EV bias). Narrow dynamic range — highlights clip easily, pay extra attention to highlight exposure. Golden-hour-biased rendering with warm midtones, clean highlight roll-off, deep saturated primaries, and a subtle cool bias in the shadows (scan artifact from Kodachrome's unique dye structure).
