# Fuji Soft Ember (Classic Neg) — Creator-Tuned Lightroom Preset

A warm, soft "ember" take on Fujifilm Classic Negative — designed for golden-hour and warm-light portraits. Emphasizes creamy skin, warm amber midtones, and muted cool shadows.

- Warm WB (6500K/+5) with soft contrast (-15) and lifted shadows (+40)
- Classic Negative green shift: Green H +25, Green Sat -30 for olive foliage
- Ember warmth: Orange/Yellow Sat +20 each, Red Sat +15
- 30pt Vib(35)-Sat(5) gap for selective midtone pop
- Blue Sky: H -20, Lum -15 for subdued teal sky
- Calibration shifts: Green H+10/S-15, Blue H-15/S+5
