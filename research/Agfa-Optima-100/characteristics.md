# Agfa Optima 100 — Aesthetic Characteristics for Lightroom Presets

## The Film

Agfa Optima 100 was a German consumer-grade color negative film produced from the mid-1990s through the mid-2000s. It was Agfa-Gevaert's answer to Kodak Gold 100 and Fuji Superia 100 in the consumer film market. Known for its European color sensibility — warm golden midtones, restrained saturation, and soft contrast. This document serves as a reference for creating a Lightroom preset that emulates Optima 100.

## Core Aesthetic

### 1. European Consumer Color
- **Golden warmth** — not the saturated yellow-red of Kodak Gold, but a subtle golden "glow" in the midtones
- **Restrained saturation** — more muted than Kodak Gold or Fuji Superia equivalents
- **Soft contrast** — consumer-grade film with moderate contrast, forgiving exposure latitude
- **European palette** — colors that read as natural under Northern European light

### 2. Color Palette by Hue

| Hue | Agfa Optima 100 Rendering |
|-----|---------------------------|
| **Blue (sky)** | Warm, slightly muted blue — not the deep blue of Kodak |
| **Green (foliage)** | Warm forest green with slight yellow bias — natural European look |
| **Red** | Moderate, slightly warm orange-red |
| **Skin tones** | Warm golden, flattering for light skin — the Agfa signature |
| **Yellow/Orange** | Prominent golden-yellow warmth |
| **Neutrals** | Warm-tinted whites and greys |

### 3. Contrast & Dynamic Range
- **Low-to-moderate contrast** — consumer film design for forgiving snapshot results
- **RMS ~9-10** — fine grain for ISO 100 consumer film
- **Wide exposure latitude** — ±2 stops, characteristic of consumer color neg
- Soft highlight rolloff — highlights compress gently

### 4. The "Golden Glow"
The defining Agfa Optima characteristic is a subtle golden warmth that pervades the entire image:
- Not an aggressive color cast, but a gentle warming that feels like "golden hour" applied at 15% opacity
- This came from Agfa's proprietary dye coupler chemistry
- Particularly flattering for European landscapes, architecture, and portraits

## Comparison: Agfa Optima 100 vs Competitors

| Attribute | Agfa Optima 100 | Kodak Gold 100 | Fuji Superia 100 |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Origin | Germany | USA | Japan |
| Color balance | Golden warmth | Yellow-red warmth | Cool-neutral |
| Saturation | Restrained | Moderate-high | Moderate |
| Contrast | Low-moderate | Moderate | Moderate |
| Skin tones | Warm golden | Warm yellow | Cool-neutral |
| Best for | European landscape, portrait | General consumer | General consumer |
