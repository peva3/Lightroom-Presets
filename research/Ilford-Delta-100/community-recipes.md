## t3mujinpack Reference

**Source**: t3mujinpack — community B&W film emulation pack

Delta 100 is referenced in the t3mujinpack with these defining characteristics:
- T-grain fine-grain look — cleaner than FP4+
- Modern rendering with more linear tonal scale
- Lower contrast than Pan F but sharper than FP4+
- Grain: Amount 15, Size 20, Roughness 48

## Community Core Settings for Delta 100 Look

### Basic Panel

Delta 100 is clean, sharp, with moderate contrast:

| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Contrast | +10 to +20 | Clean, modern contrast |
| Highlights | -25 to -35 | Good highlight protection |
| Shadows | +15 to +25 | Open shadows with texture |
| Whites | 0 to +10 | Clean whites |
| Blacks | -10 to -20 | Defined blacks, not crushed |

### B&W Mix

Delta 100 has balanced panchromatic sensitivity with a modern, clean rendering:

| Channel | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Red | +5 to +15 | Gentle skin lift |
| Orange | +5 to +15 | Natural skin tones |
| Yellow | 0 to +10 | Subtle highlight warmth |
| Green | -5 to +5 | Near-neutral foliage |
| Aqua | -5 to -10 | Cool tone definition |
| Blue | -10 to -20 | Moderate sky darkening |
| Purple | 0 | Neutral |
| Magenta | 0 | Neutral |
