# Changelog

## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-31

### Added
- 30 Lightroom presets across 4 categories:
  - **Color Negative** — 8 film stock emulations (Fujifilm Classic Chrome, Kodak Portra 400/160, Kodak Gold 200, Fuji Superia X-TRA 400, Fujifilm Pro 400H, Agfa Vista 200, Kodak Ektar 100)
  - **Black & White** — 4 monochrome film emulations (Ricoh High-Contrast Monochrome, Ilford HP5 Plus, Kodak Tri-X 400, Ilford Delta 3200)
  - **Slide** — 4 slide film emulations (Kodachrome 64, Fuji Velvia 50, Fuji Astia 100F, Kodak Ektachrome E100)
  - **Creative** — 14 trending and experimental looks (Cinestill 800T, Pro-Mist Dream, Cyberpunk Neon, Polaroid SX-70, and more)

### Infrastructure
- README with full settings tables, vibe descriptions, and quick-reference index
- CONTRIBUTING guide for submitting new presets
